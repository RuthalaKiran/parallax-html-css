parallax

Parallax refers to the apparent displacement or difference in the apparent position of an object viewed along two different lines of sight. This phenomenon is commonly observed in everyday life, such as when looking out of a moving vehicle, where objects closer to you appear to move faster than those in the distance. In web design, parallax scrolling is a technique that utilizes this concept to create visually appealing and immersive websites.
